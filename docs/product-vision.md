# Trakk Product Vision

## Purpose

Trakk helps small clubs, gyms, dance schools, and instructors manage members, attendance, paid sessions, free sessions, and private lessons without relying on fragile spreadsheets.

## Problem

Many clubs need to answer simple questions quickly:

- Who attended tonight?
- Did they pay?
- Are they using a pass, membership, free trial, or private lesson package?
- How many sessions do they have left?
- Which members have stopped coming?
- Which classes are growing or shrinking?

For many small organisations, the current answer is a spreadsheet, paper sheet, or someone’s memory. That works right up until it does not.

## Product principles

1. **Fast at the door**  
   Attendance recording must be quick, mobile-friendly, and usable while people are arriving.

2. **Flexible pricing**  
   Gyms, dance schools, workshops, and private lessons all use different models. Trakk should support multiple pricing styles from the start.

3. **Simple first**  
   The MVP should work before it becomes clever. Start with members, sessions, attendance, and reports.

4. **Useful history**  
   Every attendance record should become part of a useful member history.

5. **Small club friendly**  
   The app should suit small operators who do not need enterprise bloatware wearing a tracksuit.

## Target users

- Dance school owners
- Gym owners
- Martial arts clubs
- Yoga and pilates studios
- Personal trainers
- Community clubs
- Workshop organisers

## Long-term vision

Trakk becomes a simple operating system for session-based clubs:

- Member management
- Attendance tracking
- Pass and membership tracking
- Private lesson bookings
- Reports and retention insights
- Optional payments
- Optional member portal
